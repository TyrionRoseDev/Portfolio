## The Problem

I wanted a place to track the films I watch that actually felt fun to use, not just another generic list app. I wanted to build something with personality and make it my own.

## The Approach

I went with a retro drive-in cinema theme to make the whole experience feel nostalgic and playful. The UI uses vintage ticket stub graphics, marquee-style headers and a warm colour palette. Built with Vite and TanStack Router, with light/dark theme support and plans for smart recommendations and year-end viewing stats.
